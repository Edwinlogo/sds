📋 Basic Requirements

  • Valid Driver's License (CDL preferred for bus routes)
  • Clean driving record — last 3 years
  • Federal & state background check clearance
  • Minimum 21 years of age
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Drug screening clearance
  • Basic English communication skills
  • CPR / First Aid certified (or willing to obtain)
  • Reliable, punctual, and professional attitude
  • Prior experience with children is a plus
