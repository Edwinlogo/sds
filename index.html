Basic Requirements

  • Valid Driver's License (CDL preferred for bus routes)
  • Clean driving record (last 3 years)
  • Pass federal & state background check
  • At least 21 years of age
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Drug screening clearance
  • Basic English communication skills
  • CPR / First Aid certification (or willingness to obtain)
  • Reliable and punctual work ethic
  • Experience with children (a plus!)
